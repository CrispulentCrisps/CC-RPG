CC-RPG

Game Type: RPG

Players: SinglePlayer

Requirements:
	NPC Dialogue
	Turn Based Battles
	Cutscenes using in-engine characters
	Multiple areas
	Doors, houses and other inhabitable areas to explore
	Enemies and bosses based off of CC members and/or chiptune
	Multiple party members

Members will be done via an OPT-IN system. No user wwill be put in without their expressed consent.

this project will be available to anyone and anybody can modify it to further the project.
